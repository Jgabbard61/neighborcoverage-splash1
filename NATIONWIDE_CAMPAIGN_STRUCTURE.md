```

Wednesday: 7:00 AM - 11:00 PM (16 hours)
```

| **Call Duration (Avg)** | ≥45 seconds | Quality indicator |

### Red Flags 🚩

Monitor for these warning signs:

1. **Meta conversions ≠ Retreaver calls**
   - Action: Check webhook logs, verify tracking setup

2. **Cost per qualified call >$60**
   - Action: Pause, review targeting/creative, optimize

3. **Event Match Quality <5.0**
   - Action: Check user data parameters, improve data quality

4. **Learning Limited** status
   - Action: Increase budget or broaden targeting

5. **Call duration <30 seconds average**
   - Action: Review ad copy for misleading claims, target quality

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Targeting Tips

1. **Start Broad**: Don't over-narrow targeting. Meta's algorithm is very good at finding converters.
2. **Avoid Overlap**: If creating multiple ad sets, use audience exclusions to prevent overlap.
3. **Use Lookalikes Later**: Once you have 50+ conversions, create lookalike audiences for scaling.

### Creative Tips

1. **Mobile-First**: 80%+ of traffic will be mobile. Ensure images look good on small screens.
2. **Clear CTA**: Phone number should be VERY visible in image.
3. **Test Regularly**: Refresh creative every 2-4 weeks to combat ad fatigue.
4. **Avoid Insurance Jargon**: Speak like a neighbor, not an insurance agent.

### Budget Tips

1. **Don't Panic**: First 3-7 days are learning phase - costs will be higher.
2. **Increase Gradually**: Never increase budget by more than 30% at once.
3. **Weekend Performance**: Monitor weekend vs weekday. Some audiences shop differently.
4. **Time of Day**: If 24/7 schedule underperforms, focus on peak hours only.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "Learning Limited" Status

**Cause:** Not enough conversions per week (need 50+)

**Solutions:**
- Increase budget by 30-50%
- Broaden targeting (remove some interests)
- Consolidate ad sets (fewer ad sets = more conversions per ad set)
- Lower bid cap temporarily to allow more conversions

### High Cost Per Qualified Call (>$60)

**Possible Issues:**
1. Learning phase (normal for first 7 days)
2. Poor creative (low CTR)
3. Wrong audience (low intent users)
4. Bid cap too high

**Solutions:**
- Wait for learning phase to complete
- Test new ad images/copy
- Refine targeting (exclude low-quality behaviors)
- Lower bid cap to $40 and monitor

### Low Call Volume (<3 calls/day on $150 budget)

**Possible Issues:**
1. Budget too low
2. Ad delivery issues
3. Targeting too narrow
4. Poor ad quality/relevance

**Solutions:**
- Increase budget to $200+/day
- Check delivery insights for issues
- Broaden targeting (remove some restrictions)
- Refresh ad creative
